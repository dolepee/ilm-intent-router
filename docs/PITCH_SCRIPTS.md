## 2-minute demo talk track
1. Problem: DeFi users overpay via bad routing, high slippage, and timing mistakes.
2. Product: Intent Guard lets users specify outcome + constraints, not route details.
3. Mechanism: Multiple solvers compete; invalid quotes are filtered; best valid quote wins.
4. Enforcement: Constraints are first-class checks, not UI-only suggestions.
5. Settlement: Intent lifecycle and proof hash are written onchain.
6. Business model: small fill fee + solver participation economics + premium risk modes.
7. Why now: Agent-native execution + composable onchain markets.
